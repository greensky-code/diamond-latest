ARCSH Commands on the Menu Bar

 

Command             Menu   Description

Batch Close

S

Used to close batches with status of either Open or Error (once errors have been corrected). Batch close sums all transaction amounts and compares total to Batch Total; sums transaction items and compares total to Item Count. If item total and batch total do not match, batch will not close.

Cash Post

S

Posts the cash batch. Requires entry of GL month to which cash will be posted, and Paid Thru Date used for customers who paid in advance of their first bill.

Print Cash Batch Posting by Customer

S

Used to print a batch list sorted by customer ID.

Print Cash Posting Summary

S

Used to print a summary report of cash posted.

Print Cash Report by Customer

S

Used to list all posted premium payments not yet reflected on a premium bill.
