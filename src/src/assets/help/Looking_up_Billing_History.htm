Looking up Billing History

Restrictions

Instructions

Frequently Asked Questions

In the course of managing group and member relations, it is important to trace the events of premium billing on a group or member level in order to diagnose problems or explain billed amounts to customers. Billing history is maintained automatically, and can be viewed using BLHST.

Because premium calculations can be complicated, interpreting premium billing history can be complicated. See specific examples in Premium Billing, How DIAMOND Thinks User Documentation.

Restrictions

None.

Instructions

Step

Comment

1. Bring up BLHST on the Premium Billing menu.

 

2. Enter the group or subscriber ID for the group or subscriber whose history you want to view.

 

3. The following columns of information will be displayed on the initial screen, with data for each billing period:

  • Prior balance

  • Payments

  • Adjustments

  • New charges

  • Ending balance

 

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why doesn’t any history appear when I request this group or subscriber?

If no history appears, confirm that the group or subscriber has actually been billed. History will not appear in BLHST until one of two events has taken place:

  • the group or subscriber has been billed (and that billing has been posted), OR

  • the group or subscriber has made a payment in advance of that first bill, the customer record was manually created using ARCUS, the payment entered through ACSH, and the cash batch was posted.
