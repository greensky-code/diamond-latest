Canceling an Administrative Fee Job

Restrictions

Instructions

Frequently Asked Questions

Use the following procedure to cancel an administrative fee job.

Restrictions

None.

Instructions

Step

Comment

1. Press F3, type JOBAD, and then press ENTER.

 

2. In the picklist, click the administrative fee job you want to cancel.

 

3. Click Cancel.

 

4. On the File menu, click Run Job.

 

Frequently Asked Questions

None.
