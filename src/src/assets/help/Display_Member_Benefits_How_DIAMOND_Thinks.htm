Using Keyword DSPBN - Overview

Use keyword DSPBN (Display Benefits Accumulators) to display a member’s benefit information and current benefit status as of a specific date, including the member’s benefit rule IDs, benefit rule limits, expended benefit amounts, and remaining benefit amounts.

You select a specific benefit rule ID to display all of the member’s claims that have been processed against the benefit rule. You can also drill down to display the fields associated with a claim detail line.

An accumulator file tracks a member’s deductibles, maximums, and limits. A member’s copayment and coinsurance amounts will be accumulated only if there are out-of-pocket maximums, deductibles, or limits associated with them. Denied and held claims are not accumulated. Accumulator file values are expressed in either dollars (for example, deductibles) or numeric value (for example, quantity). The accumulator file contains one record per claim detail line, per benefit rule applied to that detail line. For example, if a claim detail line has a deductible amount of $100 and an out-of-pocket expense total of $500, then two benefit accumulator records are added to the accumulator file: one for the deductible rule ($100), and one for the out-of-pocket expense rule ($500).

When a claim is adjudicated, DIAMOND "sums up" a member’s appropriate claim benefit accumulator records based on certain parameters in the corresponding benefit rule (BRULE); such as, time frame, carryover, all groups, and member/family.

A members benefit history varies depending on the benefit rule. For example:

  • Lifetime Maximum Rule

All accumulated records for this rule are included in the benefit history calculation.

  • Annual Family Deductible Rule

Accumulated records for all family members for the current year are included in the benefit history calculation.

  • Copayment Rule

Accumulated records based on specific BRULE attributes are included in the benefit history calculation; such as, Med Defs, Reason Code, Time Frame + No. Years/Days, Carryover + No. Years/Days, Member/Family, and Other Med Defs.

Non-Renewable Benefits

The date you enter in the As of Date box in keyword DSPBN is the ending date for the non-renewable benefit accumulated amounts displayed in the Benefits Accumulated As Of focus box (Accum Amt). Non-renewable benefit rule "limits" (BRULE - Time Frame) dictate DIAMOND’s As of Date search logic as outlined below:

Limits (BRULE-Time Frame)

As of Date Search Logic (DSPBN-As of Date)

Calendar Year

Member benefits from January 1 of the current year through the date you entered in the As of Date box accumulate and display.

Contract Year

If a benefit start date exists on the member’s eligibility record (see MEMBR > Ben Start Date, MELIG > Ben Start Date), member benefits from the benefit start date through the date you entered in the As of Date box accumulate and display.

If a benefit start date does not exist on the member’s eligibility record, DIAMOND accumulates and displays member benefits from the member’s associated group contract effective date (see GRUPC > Effective Date) through the date you entered in the As of Date box.

See benefit rule (BRULE Type 00, Time Frame, Contract Yr.) user documentation for additional information.

Elapsed Days or Years

DIAMOND calculates a benefits accumulation start date by subtracting the BRULE > Elapsed Days or Years from the date you entered in the As of Date box. DIAMOND then accumulates and displays member benefits from the calculated date through the date you entered in the As of Date box.

For example: As of Date = 6/30/95. BRULE = 90 elapsed days. DIAMOND subtracts 90 days from 6/30/95 and arrives at a benefits accumulation start date of 4/1/95. DIAMOND accumulates and displays member benefits from 4/1/95 through 6/30/95.

Carryover

DIAMOND calculates the limit method and subtracts the number of carryover days or years to arrive at a benefits accumulation start date. DIAMOND then accumulates and displays member benefits from the calculated date through the date you entered in the As of Date box.

Lifetime

All member benefits through the date you entered in the As of Date box accumulate and display.

 

Note

If any claims are not displayed (included) due to security reasons, An informational "Some claims may not display due to security privileges" message displays. For more information about security, see Users (SUSER) user documentation.

Renewable Benefits

As with non-renewable benefits, the As of Date you enter in keyword DSPBN is the ending date for the renewable benefit accumulated amounts displayed in the Benefits Accumulated As Of focus box (Accum Amt). Although the As of Date search logic is the same as for non-renewable benefits, DIAMOND calculates the "renewal period" (based on the as of date entered). Only the benefit amounts that have accumulated during the benefit renewal period display. However, DIAMOND displays all of the claim detail lines (in the Benefit Detail focus box) that are associated with the renewable benefit based on the As of Date and the BRULE-Time Frame. As a result, the claim detail lines displayed in the DSPBN - Benefit Detail focus box (for the renewable benefit) may not total up to the amount displayed in the Benefits Accumulated As Of focus box.

For example:

BRULE-Time Frame = Calendar Year (1)

BRULE-Renewal = Elapsed Days (30 - From Discharge Date)

DSPBN-As of Date = 4/1/00

Any benefit amounts incurred from 3/2/00 - 3/31/00 accumulate and display the total in the Benefits Accumulated As Of focus box (Accum Amt).

DIAMOND displays all benefit claim detail lines/amounts incurred from 1/1/00 - 4/1/00 in the Benefit Detail focus box.

See Benefit Rules and Packages - Benefit Renewal Periods for benefit renewal information.

Displaying Benefit Detail

All claim detail lines associated with the benefit rule ID selected in the Benefits Accumulated As Of focus box display, based on the As of Date entered and the BRULE-Time Frame.

If necessary, you can drill down to the data fields associated with a displayed claim detail line. Select the desired claim detail line in this focus box, and click the Select Clm button. Claim header and claim detail line data for the selected claim line displays.

Click the Pat Liab button or select Special menu> Patient Liabilities to view underlying

  • Copay

  • Coinsurance

  • Limits (Not Covered)

  • Deductible

  • Exclusions (Not Covered)

Benefit rules/amounts for claim detail lines having more than one benefit rule of the same benefit rule type; that is, DSPBN Claim Display window fields Not Cov Rsn, Copay Rsn, Co-ins Rsn and/or Deduct Rsn are filled with asterisks.

Important

You cannot view the processing messages associated with a claim from DSPBN - Select Claim. Use PSCLM, INCLM, or DNCLM to view processing messages.
