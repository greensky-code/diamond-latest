DIAMOND ID

O

Other Family Coverage

R

Indicator of how the coverage information entered in this record pertains to other family members.

Selected from predefined list

Valid values are:

All - All family members also covered

Exclude - Certain family members are excluded from this coverage

Include - Certain family members are included in this coverage

None - No other family members are covered

The default is None. If the entry is either Exclude or Include, Person Number is required.

Warning: Automatic COB Order of Liability (COBOR) assignment may give unreliable results if you use Relationship as a determinant in COBOR and select All, Include or Exclude here. If you want to use Relationship as a determinant in COBOR rules, instead select None here and create separate individual COBHS records for dependents.

P/N (3 occurrences)

C

The person numbers of the other family members who are either Included or Excluded according to the entry in Other Fam Cov above.

Manually entered.

P/N is required if Other Fam Cov above is either E or I; it is not used if A or N.

Payment Policy

O

COB payment policy for this commercial other carrier

Selected from predefined list

This field is enabled when the Other Carrier’s Carrier Type (from CARCD) = Commercial, Other or any other type which is not Medicare.

Informational field only – no associated processing logic. You can use Payment Policy as a COB Hold/Deny determinant.

Payment Policy codes are maintained in keyword CODES with system code type COBPYMT.

Predefined valid values:

Pay & Pursue - The Other Carrier pays claims for this group and then pursues potential payment recovery from another payor if appropriate.

Pursue & Pay - The Other Carrier holds or denies claims for this group and then pursues potential recovery from another payor. When a payment for a claim is received from another payor or it is determined that an another payor payment does not exist, the Other Carrier normally releases the hold or deny conditions for the claim and pays it.

Modified Pursue & Pay - For their subscribers, the Other Carrier applies the Pay and Pursue process. For dependents, the Pursue and Pay process.

None - No formal policy exists for COB payments.
