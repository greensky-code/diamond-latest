Delete System-Generated Detail Lines from an Institutional Claim

Use the following procedure to delete all unfinalized system-generated detail lines from an institutional claim. System-generated detail lines originate from Whole Claim Pricing.


To delete system-generated detail lines from an institutional claim

  1. Press F3, type INCLM, and then press ENTER.

  2. Display the claim header for which you want to delete all system-generated detail lines.

  3. On the Special menu, click Remove System Generated Lines. All unfinalized system-generated payable detail lines are deleted.
