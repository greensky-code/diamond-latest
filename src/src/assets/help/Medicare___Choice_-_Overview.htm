Medicare + Choice - Overview

A Medicare + Choice plan is a health benefits coverage offered under a policy or contract offered by a Medicare + Choice organization, under which a specific set of health benefits are offered at a uniform premium and uniform level of cost-sharing to all Medicare beneficiaries residing in the service area of the Medicare + Choice plan.

DIAMOND Medicare + Choice logic enables you to:

  • Add or change member Medicare + Choice information, including entitlement information, demographic and plan benefit package (PBP) information, and institutional data

  • Validate that the effective date for a member's eligibility period for a Medicare + Choice plan line of business is equal to or later than the member's Medicare Part A effective date and Medicare Part B enrollment date

  • Validate that the effective date for a member's eligibility period for a Medicare + Choice plan line of business is the first of the month

  • Validate that the termination date for a member's eligibility period for a Medicare + Choice plan line of business is the first of the month following the Medicare Part A/B termination date

To set up Medicare + Choice functionality, see Setting Up Medicare + Choice functionality.
