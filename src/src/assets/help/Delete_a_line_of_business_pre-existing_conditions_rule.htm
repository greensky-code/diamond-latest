Delete a line of business pre-existing conditions rule

You cannot delete a keyword LBPEC (Line of Business Pre-Existing Conditions) pre-existing condition rule once it has been saved. You also cannot delete a default pre-existing condition rule from a line of business pre-existing conditions rule.

However, you can use keyword LBPEC to limit the effective period of a default pre-existing condition rule for a line of business pre-existing conditions rule to a single day.

To limit the effective period of a default pre-existing condition rule for a line of business pre-existing conditions rule to a single day

  1. Type LBPEC and press ENTER.

  2. In the Line of Business box, type the line of business code of the rule for which you want to limit a default pre-existing condition rule.

  3. In the PEC Rule ID and effective period picklist, click the PEC Rule ID effective period you want to limit.

  4. In the Term Date field, type a date equal to the Effective Date.

  5. Press CTRL+S to save.
