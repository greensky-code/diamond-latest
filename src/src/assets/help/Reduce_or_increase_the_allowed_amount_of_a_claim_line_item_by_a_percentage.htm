Reduce or increase the allowed amount of a claim line item by a percentage

Use the following procedure to reduce or increase the allowed amount of a claim line item by a percentage.

To reduce or increase the allowed amount of a claim line item by a percentage

  1. Press F3, type PSCLM, and then press ENTER.

  2. Display the claim you want to change.

  3. On the Topic menu, click Detail File.

  4. On the Special menu, click Allowed Factor.

  5. In the Allowed Factor box, type the percentage you want to reduce or increase the allowed amount for the first Modifier code.

  6. If necessary, in the Sec. Allowed Factor, type percentage you want to reduce or increase the allowed amount for the second Modifier code.

  7. Click OK.
