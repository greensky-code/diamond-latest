Delete a member condition

You cannot delete a keyword MCOND (Member Conditions) member condition once the record is saved. However, you can use keyword MCOND to limit the effective period of the member condition record to a single day.

To limit the effective period of a member condition to a single day

  1. Press F3, type MCOND, and then press ENTER.

  2. In the DIAMOND ID box, type the DIAMOND ID of the member for whom you want to limit the effective period of a member condition record.

  3. In the Subscriber ID box, type the Subscriber ID of the member for whom you want to limit the effective period of a member condition record.

  4. In the person number picklist, click the person number of the member for whom you want to limit the effective period of a member condition record.

  5. In the member condition picklist, click the member condition record you want to limit.

  6. In the Term Date field, type a date equal to the Effective Date.

  7. Press CTRL+S to save.
