Heads Down Claims Valid Prism Direction Combinations

Four fields are used to define the prism for each eye (OD and OS): Prism No. (1), Prism Direc (1), Prism No. (2), Prism Direc (2).

Valid prism direction combinations for Prism Direc (1) and Prism Direc (2) for each eye include:

Up and In

or

In and Up

Up and Out

or

Out and Up

Up and Blank

 

 

Down and In

or

In and Down

Down and Out

or

Out and Down

Down and Blank

 

 

In and Blank

 

 

Out and Blank

 

 

Blank and Blank
