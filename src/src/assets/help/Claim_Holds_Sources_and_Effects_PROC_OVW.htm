Claim Holds: Sources and Effects

You can hold claims in several ways:

  • You can define table driven header hold rules to hold entire claims

  • You can use predefined DIAMOND hold rules to hold entire claims under certain conditions (for example, you can hold all claims for a specific member)

  • You can use predefined DIAMOND hold rules to hold individual claim detail lines under certain conditions (for example, you can use a Message and Pend benefit rule to hold individual claim lines for which the benefit rule applies)

  • You can manually hold an entire claim

  • You can manually hold an individual claim detail line

  • You can activate holds generation from third party interfaces

The following table summarizes the specific sources and effects of holds in DIAMOND.

CLHLD

User-defined Claim Header Hold rules

Hold entire claim at header level only

Use CLHLD to enter Claim Header Hold rules. You can use any claim header column (except date columns) as a determinant and specify multiple determinant values.

DIAMOND automatically applies claim header hold rules when you initially move from header to detail. You can also manually apply rules by using INCLM/PSCLM/DNCLM Special menu command Re-apply Claim Holds.

CLHLD

COBHD

User-defined COB claim hold and deny rules.

Hold entire claim – all detail lines

Use COBHD to enter COB claim hold and deny rules. You can select rule determinants from several tables: COB History, Group Detail, Member Master, Professional Services claim header, Institutional claim header, and Dental claim header.

COBHD rules supersede COB hold and deny conditions dictated by settings in the parameter COBCLAIMACTN.

GROUP - Payment Threshold (Paid Through Threshold Percent)

Group claims hold based on group premiums paid threshold percent

Hold entire claim – all detail lines

Use GROUP to enter a PTD Threshold Percent and select a Claim Hold Reason Code.

When a non-payment of premium condition applies, assigns the Claim Hold Reason Code to all associated claims dated after updated GRUPB — Paid Through date.

GROUP

GROUP - Hold Rsn/Date

Group claims hold

Hold entire claim – all detail lines

Use GROUP to select a Hold Rsn and enter an effective Date for the hold.

Holds all subsequently processed claims (for members associated with the group) with a primary date of service on or after the Group Hold Date. Assigns the Hold Rsn specified as the Hold Reason Code to all claim detail lines on the claim.

GROUP

GRUPD - Pre-Existing Conditions

Pre-existing conditions

Hold selected detail lines

On the Group Detail record (GRUPD) enter Exclusion Type (Days or Months), Exclusion Period, Apply PCP and select the desired Hold Reason code.

(Note: DIAMOND does not apply pre-existing conditions logic if the MELIG PEC Waived check box is selected.)

For example, assume an Exclusion Type of Days and an Exclusion Period of 90. DIAMOND uses these and the member’s effective date to determine the dates of the exclusion period. DIAMOND then compares that period against the claim detail line dates of service to determine if the claim is to be held due to a pre-existing condition.

If you select the Apply PCP check box, pre-existing conditions logic applies even if the provider is the member’s PCP. If the box is clear, the logic does not apply to the member’s PCP.

GRUPD

INCLM & PSCLM – Cap Fund Status

Capitation / Special Fund Distribution processing

Hold selected detail lines

(Not actually a hold condition but does prevent a claim detail line from being finalized and selected for payment – see Comments.)

Set up a Cap Fund Model using CFMOD and run a Capitation Fund Distribution batch job using CFDST. DIAMOND automatically applies corresponding logic during claims adjudication.

Not actually a hold condition in that DIAMOND does not create a hold reason record for the claim detail line nor change the Processing Status on the lines based on Cap Fund Status.

However, DIAMOND prevents any claim detail line with a Cap Fund Status of 1 or 2 from being finalized and selected for payment by Accounts Payable.

CFDST

Manual - Hold Reason records

Manual hold entry

Hold entire claim at header level only

or

Hold selected detail lines

Use the INCLM/PSCLM/DNCLM Topic Hold Reasons. To hold an entire claim, enter a hold reason record for Line #000. To hold an individual claim detail line, highlight the line and enter a hold reason record for that specific line and sub-line.

 

INCLM, PSCLM, & DNCLM

Manual – Processing Status

Manual hold entry

Hold selected detail lines

To hold an individual claim detail line, highlight the line and change the Processing Status to H - Held.

Alternatively, enter a hold reason code for the claim detail line and DIAMOND automatically changes the Processing Status to H - Held.

INCLM, PSCLM, & DNCLM

MCHRL

Authorization-claim match rules

Hold entire claim – all detail lines

Enter an auth/claim match rule with Action = Hold.

DIAMOND holds all claim detail lines for claims that satisfy the MCHRL rule.

MCHRL

MELIG – Pre-existing conditions

Pre-existing conditions

Hold selected detail lines

On the MELIG record corresponding to the desired member and eligibility period, enter a PEC End Date.

(Note: DIAMOND does not apply pre-existing conditions logic if the MELIG PEC Waived check box is selected.)

DIAMOND holds all claim detail lines for the member where the date of service is before or equal to the PEC End Date.

 

MEMBR

Member Master

Hold selected detail lines

For the desired member, select Special menu command Claim Hold Codes. Enter a Claim Hold Code and a Claim Hold Date.

DIAMOND holds all claim detail lines for the selected member where the claim detail line (From) Date is on or after the Claim Hold Date.

MEMBR

VENDR – Hold Payment Flag

Accounts Payable

Not actually a hold condition but prevents accounts payable records for this vendor from being included when the vendor check is generated – see Comments.

Select the VENDR Hold Payment Flag check box.

If you select the VENDR Hold Payment flag, the only action DIAMOND takes is to prevent accounts payable records for this vendor from being included in a check.

Selecting the Hold Payment Flag does not cause DIAMOND to create hold reason records or change the Processing Status to Held on the claim detail lines, nor is this condition explicitly reflected on the corresponding accounts payable records.

 

SUSER

Security - Claim type, claim limit or determinant by User ID (online)

Hold entire claim – all detail lines

Specify in Users (SUSER) the User ID, claim type, claim limit or determinant for which to hold all claims entered by a specific user (User ID), and assign a selected hold reason code.

 

SUSER

SUSER

Security - Claim type, claim limit or determinant by User ID (Claims Batch)

Hold entire claim – all detail lines

Online, access a claim submitted via Claims Batch. DIAMOND re-applies SUSER hold logic for your user ID. (See SUSER, Security - Claim type, claim limit or determinant by User ID (online) above)

When a claim comes in through Claims Batch, DIAMOND cannot apply the normal SUSER hold logic (claim type, claim limit or determinant). This is because this logic is specific to an SUSER User ID and there is no corresponding User ID for Claims Batch.

When you subsequently access the claim online, DIAMOND can re-apply the SUSER hold logic based on the User ID for the session.

SUSER

System Parameter

COBCLAIMACTN

Coordination of Benefits

Hold selected detail lines

In system parameter COBCLAIMACTN, enter H (Hold) in Param 1 and the desired hold reason code in Param 2.

During on-line claims entry, if the member has a primary insurance coverage (COBHS — COB Code — P Primary) other than that on the claim, DIAMOND holds the claim and assigns the hold reason code specified in COBCLAIMACTN Parameter 2 to the claim detail line.

COBHD rules supersede COB hold (and deny) conditions dictated by COBCLAIMACTN.

PARAM

System Parameter PARTIALPAY

Requires finalizing and selecting for payment of entire claim (all detail lines) at once

Hold entire claim – all detail lines

(Not actually a hold condition but does prevent all claim detail lines from being finalized and selected for payment – see Comments.)

In system parameter PARTIALPAY, enter Y(es) in Param1

Not actually a hold condition in that DIAMOND does not create a hold reason record for each claim detail line nor change the Processing Status on the lines based on PARTIALPAY.

However, when activated, if any claim detail line on the claim has a Processing Status of Held, DIAMOND prevents any claim detail line on the same claim from being finalized and selected for payment by Accounts Payable.

PARAM

System Parameter TPLCODEACT

Third party liability (TPL) hold

Hold entire claim - all detail lines

Used in conjunction with a claim’s matched authorization wherein an AUTHS - TPL Code has been entered.

This parameter specifies matching claim disposition (Hold/Deny) and disposition reason.
