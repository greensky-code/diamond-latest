About CLHLD

Use CLHLD to define rules for applying claim header holds to institutional, professional services, and dental claims. When the data on a claim header satisfies the header hold rule you set up in CLHLD, DIAMOND holds the entire claim. While the entire claim is on hold, no claim detail lines on the claim may be selected for payment by DIAMOND’s Accounts Payable function.

You may select any claim header field (except a date field) as a determinant for applying header holds. For example, you could use Vendor ID to hold all claims for a particular pending resolution of some problem.
