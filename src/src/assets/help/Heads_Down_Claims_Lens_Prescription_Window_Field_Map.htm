This window is used to enter lens prescription data in addition to claims data entered on the claims header and detail windows. You may enter a claims header and lens prescription data only (no claim detail data) or you may enter claim header, claim detail, and lens prescription data.

Warning

Some of the entry fields are not validated when you enter lens prescription data using this window. However, the fields have been documented here to indicate the validations that DIAMOND performs when you enter lens prescription data online using the Professional Services Claims (PSCLM) window.

Fields are listed alphabetically by focus box.
