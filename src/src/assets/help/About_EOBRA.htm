About EOBRA

Use keyword EOBRA (EOB/RA Printing) to create a file to print explanations of benefits (EOBs) and remittance advices (RAs). You can also view the results of these print requests.

To print EOBs or RAs, use Intelligent Query™ (IQ) reporting software and the EOB/RA IQ templates maintained in keyword BANKA (Bank Account).

Whether EOBs and RAs are printed depends on entries in keywords LINBS (Line of Business), PROVC (Provider Contract), and the parameter DFLTPRINTSTAT.

Claim detail lines with a status "Hold" do not display on RAs or EOBs.
