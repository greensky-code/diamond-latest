Paying a claim to the subscriber instead of the provider

Restrictions

Instructions

Frequently Asked Questions

Use the following procedure to pay a claim to the subscriber instead of the provider.

Restrictions

The claim must be unposted (open) to pay a claim to the subscriber instead of the provider.

Instructions

Step

Comment

1. In keyword INCLM, PSCLM, or DNCLM, display the non-final claim for which you want to pay the subscriber.

 

2. On the Topic menu, click Header File.

 

3. On the Special menu, click Pay Subscriber.

The Claim Header Pay Subscriber dialog box displays.

4. Click to select the Pay Subscriber check box, and then click OK.

 

5. On the File menu, click Save.

A vendor record is created in keyword VENDR (Vendor Master) with the Vendor ID prefixed with "S."

Frequently Asked Questions

None.
