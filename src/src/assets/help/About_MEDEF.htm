About MEDEF

Use keyword MEDEF (Medical Definitions) to define medical definitions, categories of medical services such as office visits and lab tests.

During claims adjudication, DIAMOND processing looks for a match between the medical definition determinant values and the information on the claim. When the match is found a medical definition is assigned to the claim detail line.

This assigned medical definition drives the claim adjudication process, capitation processing for stoploss and deduction pools, and general ledger assignments, as well as being useful for reporting.
