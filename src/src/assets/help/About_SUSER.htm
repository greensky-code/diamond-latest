About SUSER

Use keyword SUSER (Users) to do the following:

  • Add, change, or delete users

  • Add, change, or delete super users

  • Add, change, or delete security templates

  • Assign or change user passwords

  • Change the DIAMONDUPDATE role password

  • Limit a user's access to records associated with specific groups, providers, provider panels, vendors, IPAs, or lines of business.

  • Limit a security template's access to records associated with specific groups, providers, provider panels, vendors, IPAs, or lines of business.
