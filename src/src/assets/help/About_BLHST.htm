About BLHST

Use keyword BLHST (Display Premium Billing History) to view posted bills and accounts receivable customer information, including adjustments, detailed member level invoice information, and combined group summary records.

Keyword BLHST has several windows for viewing increasingly detailed information. Payments and adjustments are shown in the order of invoice number on which the information (billed amounts, payments and adjustments) appeared.
