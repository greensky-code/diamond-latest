PCPJB Commands on the Menu Bar

 

Command             Menu   Description

PCP Job Setup Selection

S

Use to include or exclude lines of business in the PCPJB job. If you specify only lines of business to include, DIAMOND excludes all other lines of business. If you specify only lines of business to exclude, DIAMOND includes all other lines of business.

If you specify some lines of business to include and others to exclude, DIAMOND excludes all lines of business not specifically included. In addition, you can specify that DIAMOND also exclude a line of business that falls within the included range.

For example:

From Thru

Include AAA DDD

Exclude ABC

Exclude EEE EFA

 

Result: All lines of business from AAA through DDD are included except ABC, since the ABC exclusion falls within the included range. The Exclude EEE through EFA exclusion is not needed. Since DIAMOND excludes all lines of business not specifically included when some are included and some are excluded, DIAMOND would have excluded EEE through EFA anyway.
