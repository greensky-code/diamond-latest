About INCLM

Use keyword INCLM (Institutional Claims) to enter, update, price, and adjudicate institutional claims, including primary care, specialist, vision, and other professional services.

Keyword INCLM has multiple windows, including the header window, which pertains to the entire claim, the detail window, which itemizes each service provided, and others.

You can change member, vendor, and provider data on a claim that has not been finalized. Claims are finalized by running an A/P update job in keyword APUPD.
