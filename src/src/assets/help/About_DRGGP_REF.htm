About DRGGP

Use keyword DRGGP (DRG Grouper/Pricer Maintenance) to add, change, and delete DRG Pricer records used for institutional claims (INCLM) processing. DIAMOND uses DRG pricer records for internal DRG pricing only. DIAMOND does not use DRG pricer records for DRG pricing with an external DRG pricer.

You can also view existing DRG Grouper records in DRGGP. DRG Grouper records are no longer needed for DRG grouping in DIAMOND and cannot be added or edited.

For more information, see DRG Grouping and Pricing, How DIAMOND Thinks, Setting Up the HSS Optimizer and The Daemon and EPI Utilities – Overview.
