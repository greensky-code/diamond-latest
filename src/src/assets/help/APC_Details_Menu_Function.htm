APC Details Menu Command

Field Name

Req

Description

Source

Comments

APC Code

D

Displays the APC code.

APC Grouper

5-alphanumeric characters.

Co-payment

D

Displays the patient co-insurance amount for this APC, wage-adjusted.

APC Pricer

The amounts in the Co-payment, Line Item Deductible, Line Item Outlier, and Payment fields are added to figure an amount to display in the Allowed Amt box on the claims detail window.

Discount Factor

D

Displays the discount, if any, applied to the reimbursement calculated, for the corresponding procedure APC.

APC Pricer

 

Line Item Deductible

D

Displays the portion of the claim deductible, which was reallocated to this claim detail line.

APC Pricer

The amounts in the Co-payment, Line Item Deductible, Line Item Outlier, and Payment fields are added to figure an amount to display in the Allowed Amt box on the claims detail window.

Line Item Outlier

D

Displays the outlier payment for this claim detail line.

APC Pricer

The amounts in the Co-payment, Line Item Deductible, Line Item Outlier, and Payment fields are added to figure an amount to display in the Allowed Amt box on the claims detail window.

Line Item Packaged Chgs

D

Displays the charges from other packaged lines that were allocated to this claim for outlier pricing (4/1/2002 and later).

APC Pricer

 

Payment

D

Displays the Medicare payment for the corresponding APC, wage-adjusted, after any applicable discount, including any transitional pass-thru payments, not including coinsurance payment or patient deductible, not including any outlier payments.

APC Pricer

The amounts in the Co-payment, Line Item Deductible, Line Item Outlier, and Payment fields are added to figure an amount to display in the Allowed Amt box on the claims detail window.

Payment Status

D

Displays the payment status.

APC Pricer

Payment status comes from the APC Pricer and can be the following.

First byte:

  • A - Other payment methodology. The affected claim detail line(s) are denied and the Allowed Amt box is set to $.00 on the claim detail window.

  • B - Not allowed, not paid under APCs. The affected claim detail line(s) are denied and the Allowed Amt box is set to $.00 on the claim detail window.

  • C - Inpatient service, not paid under APCs. The affected claim detail line(s) are denied and the Allowed Amt box is set to $.00 on the claim detail window.

  • E - Non-covered service, not paid under APCs. The affected claim detail line(s) are denied and the Allowed Amt box is set to $.00 on the claim detail window.

  • F - Corneal tissue acquisition. The affected claim detail line(s) are denied and the Allowed Amt box is set to $.00 on the claim detail window.

  • G - Current drug/biological with transitional pass-thru

  • H - Hi cost device with transitional pass-thru

  • J - New drug/biological with transitional pass-thru (prior to 4/1/2002)

  • K - Drug, not eligible for pass-through payments

  • N - Packaged/In-cidental, no additional payment

  • P - Partial hospitalization service paid as per-diem

  • S - Ambulatory procedure, no discounting

  • T - Ambulatory procedure, subject to discounting

  • V - Medical visit

  • X - Ancillary service

Total APC Details

D

Displays the sum of the amounts in the Payment, Co-payment, Line Item Deductible, and Line Item Outlier boxes.

Displayed

 

Rate

D

Displays the adjusted payment rate for the corresponding procedure APC, after labor adjustment, before discounting.

APC Pricer

 

Surg. Proc. Chgs Reallocated

D

Displays the charges from all surgical procedures on the claim reallocated based on the percentage distribution of the APC payments for those items (4/1/2002 and later).

APC Pricer

 

Weight

D

Displays the weight associated with this APC, if used, with a base rate to calculate reimbursement.
