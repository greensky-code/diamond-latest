View a claim

Use the following procedure to view an institutional, professional services, or dental claim.

To view a claim

  1. Press F3, type CLDSP, and then press ENTER. The Member Utilization Display dialog box displays.

  2. Enter member data. F5 lookup is available. If From Date of service is selected, all claims following the date entered are selected for viewing. Leave the date blank to select all claims entered for that member.

  3. In the picklist, click a claim to display the claim header and line item detail.

Tip

To choose another member, click Choose Member.
