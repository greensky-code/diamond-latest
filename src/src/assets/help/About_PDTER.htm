About PDTER

Some institutional provider contracts have different reimbursement arrangements depending on the type of service rendered. For example, a health plan may reimburse a hospital on a per diem basis for medical/surgical inpatient stays, but on a per case basis for inpatient detoxification stays. This kind of reimbursement requires whole claim, rather than line-by-line, pricing. To apply different whole claim pricing logic to different kinds of claims from the same provider, DIAMOND must be able to distinguish different claim types.

Use PDTER to tell DIAMOND how to recognize different claim types depending on the characteristics of the claim, such as the place of service, bill code, or diagnosis, and use this information to select the correct whole claim price rules.
