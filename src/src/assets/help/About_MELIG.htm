About MELIG

Use keyword MELIG (Member Eligibility History) to view subscriber and family member periods of eligibility, typically as part of using the Member Master (MEMBR) record. A period of eligibility is created when a member is added or when an existing member’s time-dependent eligibility data is changed. Eligibility history records accumulate as members change enrollment from one benefit plan to another, change their primary care provider (PCP), change their rider coverage, change their premium override amounts, and so forth.

MELIG can be used to update retroactive periods of eligibility. Use the Special menu in MEMBR for all changes where the effective date of the change is equal to or greater than the effective date of the most current eligibility history record. For more information, see Membership and Eligibility> Changes to Member Records user documentation.
