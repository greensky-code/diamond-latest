About ARCSH

Use keyword ARCSH (A/R Cash Receipts) to record the receipt of premium payments from customers, both groups and individuals, close cash receipt batches, and post batches.

As a result of posting cash batches, fields related to "Paid Through" dates and "Claim Hold Reason" dates may be updated for groups and individual members.
