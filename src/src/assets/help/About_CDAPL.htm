About CDAPL

Use keyword CDAPL (Claims Detail Authorization Procedure Link) to define rules for performing authorization/claim detail linking and pricing. You define CDAPL rules for a specific line of business, authorization type and effective date combination.

When you enter claim detail lines for a claim that has a matching authorization on the claim header window and also matches a CDAPL rule line of business/authorization type/effective dates combination, DIAMOND searches for matching authorization detail records. DIAMOND then applies the CDAPL rules to the each claim detail line and matching authorization procedure detail record to determine what action to take on each claim detail line.
