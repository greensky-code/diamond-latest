Vendor 1099 Flag

R

The format in which the 1099 is to be sent to the entity.

Selected from predefined list

Valid selections are:

No 1099

Corporate format

Individual format

When this field is set to Corporate or Personal Format, DIAMOND flags subsequent vendor accounts payable (claims) records as "tax reportable." Only tax reportable accounts payable records are included during vendor 1099-MISC IRS tax form production.

If, during the year, this field is changed from Corporate or Personal Format to No 1099, subsequent vendor accounts payable (claims) records will not be flagged as tax reportable and will not be included during vendor 1099-MISC IRS tax form production for that year.
