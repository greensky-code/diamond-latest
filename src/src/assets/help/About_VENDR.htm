About VENDR

Use keyword VENDR (Vendor Master) to add vendors and maintain vendor data. Vendors are also known as payees. There can be one-to-one, one-to-many, many-to-many, or many-to-one relationships between providers and vendors. For example, an individual provider may receive payments directly; in which case the vendor and provider are the same (although there are both a vendor record and a provider record for this same entity). Alternatively, several providers can be members of a single medical group, which is the business entity for those providers, in which case each of these several providers is associated with a single vendor record.

You cannot add a Vendor ID prefixed with "S." A Vendor ID prefixed with "S" indicates a vendor created to make payments to a subscriber. A Vendor ID prefixed with "SD" indicates a vendor created to make payments to the dependent of a subscriber. Subscriber and Dependent vendor records are created automatically by DIAMOND processing.
