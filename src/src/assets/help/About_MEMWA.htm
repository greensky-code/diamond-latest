About MEMWA

Use keyword MEMWA (Member Working Aged) to store information about working aged surveys sent to member. You can store the date a survey was sent to a member and the date a response was received, as well as the effective and termination date of the survey itself. You can also store additional data based on your business requirements by using the user defined fields provided.
