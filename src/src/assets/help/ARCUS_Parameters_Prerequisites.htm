ARCUS Parameters & Prerequisites

DIAMOND does not edit manually entered customer information against the corresponding GROUP and MEMBR records. Therefore, special care must be taken when manually adding or changing customer records. If there are differences between the information in the GROUP and MEMBR records and the corresponding customer record information, DIAMOND will consider the customer record unrelated to the GROUP and MEMBR data, and an additional customer record will be created automatically to match the GROUP and MEMBR information.
