About ADDSP

Use keyword ADDSP (Administrative Fee Display) to display an administrative fee for a claim. Administrative fees are viewable in keyword ADDSP after you have posted an administrative fee job in keyword JOBAD (Administrative Fee Batch Job).

After displaying an administrative fee for a claim, you can then display the corresponding accounts payable transaction record in keyword ACPAY (Accounts Payable).
