Adjust Intr./
Pnlty/Dscnt. Reason

D

Displays the reason code for the manually entered interest, penalty, or discount amount applied to an adjusted claim detail line.

Displayed.

A value is displayed here only if the Reason For Adjust field is filled in on the Adjust Interest/Penalty/Discount Amount special topic that is available from adjusted claim detail lines.

Deduct Some Unclean Days

O

Specify the number of days to deduct from the unclean days count for this claim line.

Manually entered.

This field is not available if the Override All Unclean Days field on this command is checked.

You must fill in this field prior to running APUPD, otherwise, the information entered here will not affect the interest calculation.

Discount Amount

D

Displays the discount amount subtracted from the payment for this claim line.

Displayed

This field remains blank until check post is run.

Interest Amount

D

Displays the interest amount paid on this claim line.

Displayed.

This field remains blank until check post is run.

Interest Inquiry Date

O

Override date for interest calculations.

Manually entered.

If you supply a date in this field, it will be used as the calculation date in interest calculations instead of either the claim’s Received Date or the Interest Reset Date.

Must be greater than or equal to the Received Date.

If the date you enter here is greater than the pay date, interest will not be calculated for this claim line.

You must fill in this field prior to running APUPD, otherwise, the information entered here will not affect the interest calculation.

Interest Reset Date

O

Override date for interest calculations.

Manually entered.

If you supply a date in this field, and the Use Reset Date field in the LINBS screen is checked, and the Interest Inquiry Date on this command screen is blank, DIAMOND uses the Interest Reset Date as the Calculation Date in interest calculations.

Must be greater than or equal to the Received Date.

If the date you enter here is greater than the pay date, interest will not be calculated for this claim line.

You must fill in this field prior to running APUPD, otherwise, the information entered here will not affect the interest calculation.

Interest Unclean Days

D

The number of unclean interest days for this claim line.

Displayed.

Displays interest unclean days that the claim accumulated while it was in the DDS API/EDI staging tables.

After you run check post, this field is updated to display the total of any interest unclean days accumulated in the staging tables and interest unclean days accumulated in DIAMOND’s production tables.
