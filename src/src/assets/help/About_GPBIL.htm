About GPBIL

Use keyword GPBIL (Premium Billing Setup) to initiate new billing runs or to calculate, post or cancel outstanding billing runs.

Premium billing is completed in two steps, allowing verification of the accuracy of the premium bill records prior to posting them to the general ledger accounts. If necessary, a job can be cancelled after a bill calculation run so that appropriate adjustments can be made to eligibility information and a new bill calculation job submitted. Once a premium billing run has been posted, no changes can be made to it.
