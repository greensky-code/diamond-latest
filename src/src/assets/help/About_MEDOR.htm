About MEDOR

Use keyword MEDOR (Alternate Search Order) to define alternate search medical definition rules and an alternate search order of applying those rules.

An alternate search medical definition rule is defined in keyword MEDOR by linking a selected alternate search criterion (such as, line of business) with a specific medical definition rule and its determinants (e.g., place of service, diagnosis). In this way, for example, a specific medical definition that is valid only for a certain line of business may be applied strictly for that line of business.
