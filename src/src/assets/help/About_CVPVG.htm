About CVPVG

Use CVPVG to add, change, or delete the "covering" (patient care backup) providers for Covering Provider IDs entered in CVPVC. Physicians may arrange to have other physicians cover their patients for them when they are unavailable. Specialists usually cover for other specialists and Primary Care Physicians (PCPs) usually cover for other PCPs.
