Add member working aged survey information

Use this procedure to add member working aged survey information.

To add member working aged survey information

  1. Press F3, type MEMWA, and then press ENTER.

  2. Fill in the fields on the window. For more information, see MEMWA Field Definitions.

  3. Press CTRL+S to save.
