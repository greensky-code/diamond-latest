Changing payment of a claim from the dependent to the subscriber

Restrictions

Instructions

Frequently Asked Questions

Use the following procedure to change payment of a claim from the dependent to the subscriber.

Restrictions

The claim must be unposted (open) to change payment from the dependent to the subscriber.

Instructions

Step

Comment

1. In keyword INCLM, PSCLM, or DNCLM, display the non-final claim for which you want to change payment from the dependent to the subscriber.

 

2. On the Topic menu, click Header File.

 

3. On the Special menu, click Pay Dependent.

The Claim Header Pay Dependent dialog box displays.

4. Click to clear the Pay Dependent check box, and then click OK.

 

5. On the Special menu, click Pay Subscriber.

 

6. Click to select the Pay Subscriber check box, and then click OK.

The Claim Header Pay Subscriber dialog box displays.

7. On the File menu, click Save.

 

Frequently Asked Questions

None.
