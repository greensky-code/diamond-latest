GRUPC Field Definitions

Field Name  Req Description Source Comments

Group ID

R

Unique identifier of a group.

Selected from a predefined list

 

Effective Date

R

Date upon which this contract is effective.

Manually entered.

Both Effective Date and Term Date are required for accurate accumulation of Contract Year benefits.

Term Date

O

Last date this contract is effective.

Manually entered.

Both Effective Date and Term Date are required for accurate accumulation of Contract Year benefits.

Term Reason

O

Optional explanation of the termination date above.

Selected from a predefined list

Reason codes are maintained in REASN, and must have a type code TM.

Broker

O

Name of this group’s broker.

Manually entered.

 

Sales Rep

O

Name of this group’s sales representative.

Manually entered.

 

Open Enroll Start

O

First date of the next open enrollment period.

Manually entered.

 

Open Enroll End

O

Last date of the next open enrollment period.

Manually entered.

 

No. of Employees

O

Total number of employees.

Manually entered.

This field may be used to calculate penetration reports.

Waiting Period

O

Number of days a new employee must wait until eligible for benefits.

Manually entered.

 

Student Age From

O

Lower age limit for dependents who may maintain eligibility under a parent subscriber’s coverage so long as the dependent remains a full time student.

Manually entered.

If the member’s age as of the date of service is greater than the value entered here (and less than Student Age To), the member’s relationship code represents a child or other dependent, AND the primary date on the claim is greater than the Verified Through Date on the member record, A claims warning is issued.

Student Age To

O

Maximum age a student can reach and still be covered under a parent subscriber’s coverage.

Manually entered.

See note in Student Age From.

Contract Type

O

Indicator of contract type.

Manually entered.

This Contract Type is informational only, and not verified or maintained in any file.

User Date 1-10

O

Date associated with installation specific field.

Manually entered.

The label for this field is controlled by UDTXT.

Must be a valid date.

User Defined 1 - 10

O

Installation specific field.

Manually entered.

The label for this field is controlled by UDTXT.
