About PROVC

Use keyword PROVC (Provider Contracts) to add or change provider contract information. A provider may have multiple contract records depending on line of business, panel, IPA, and date.

In cases where many providers share the same contract, and you do not want to enter contract records for each individual provider, you can designate a provider a default and set up contract information which will apply to a set of providers for a specific line of business, panel, or IPA.

In addition to contract pricing details, the provider contract record also includes the following for the provider:

  • Panel and IPA affiliation

  • Covering provider group affiliation

  • Capitation vendor ID and address

  • Participating status

  • Coordination of benefits allowed amount rule

  • Cap/special fund model and submodel IDs
