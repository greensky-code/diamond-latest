About VENCR

Use keyword VENCR (Vendor Credit) to do any of the following:

  • Store information about a check the vendor has returned until there is sufficient time to research and adjust any claims as necessary.

  • Offset a credit balance, whether it is created by an adjustment, a reversal, or an advance payment.

  • Move an advance payment balance from one Vendor/Vendor Address relationship to another.
