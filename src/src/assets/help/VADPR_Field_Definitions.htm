Offset By

 

Select the method to apply the credit balance during the check print job calculation.

Selected from predefined list

The following values are available:

  • Vendor Only

Offsets against the advance payment are based on the vendor.

  • Vendor + Provider

The offsets will be any claim for that vendor and the provider entered in the Provider ID field in keyword VADPY (Vendor Advance Payment Account).

  • Vendor + Provider + Member

The offsets will be any claim that is for that vendor, the provider entered in the Provider ID field, and the member entered in the Member ID field in keyword VADPY.

  • Vendor + Member

The offsets will be any claim that is for that vendor and the member entered in the Member ID field in keyword VADPY.

Zero Payment Option

 

Select the action to take when a credit balance is reduced to zero or near-zero.

Selected from predefined list

  • Remain to General Account

When a claim payment exceeds the credit balance available in the advance payment account, the claim will be paid from the general account that is used for standard claims processing.

  • Below Zero

When a claim payment exceeds the credit balance available in the advance payment account, and the VADPR Replenish box for this account is checked, the claim payment will be held in a finalized state until there is enough money in the advance payment account to cover the amount of the claim payment.

 

Note: If you select Below Zero for the Zero Payment Option, you are required to check the Replenish box for this account.
