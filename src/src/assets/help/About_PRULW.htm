About PRULW

Use keyword PRULW (Whole Claim Price Rule) to set up and maintain the rules that calculate allowed amounts for institutional claims, which are priced as a whole, at the entire claim, level rather than line-by-line.

Within PRULW there are three rule types:

  • Base Rules calculate the primary allowed amount for the claim.

  • Outlier Rules calculate the amount payable (in addition to the primary allowed amount) when a claim exceeds a pre-defined threshold.

  • Cap Rules calculate the difference between the claim amount and the cap limit when the claim amount is higher that the cap limit. The difference is then added as a negative amount in an adjustment line to bring the total for the claim back down to the cap limit.
