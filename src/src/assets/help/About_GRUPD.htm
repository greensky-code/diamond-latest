About GRUPD

Use GRUPD to assign plans and riders to groups, and to maintain group pricing and pre-existing condition information. As members are added to a group, DIAMOND validates the member record using the group detail records. When claims are entered for members associated with this group, DIAMOND uses the benefit package information on the group detail record to apply appropriate benefit rules and, if applicable, group detail pricing.
