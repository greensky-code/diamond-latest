Change a member privacy address

Use this procedure to change a member privacy address. You can use member privacy addresses for third party ad hoc reporting, ad hoc correspondence, and EOBs.

Also, under certain conditions, DIAMOND logic uses member privacy addresses as the mailing address for claims payments for Pay to Subscriber and Pay to Dependent claims. For more information, see the Member Conditions, How DIAMOND Thinks section entitled Using the Privacy Address for Pay to Subscriber and Pay to Dependent Claims.

To change a member privacy address

  1. Press F3, type ALIAS, and then press ENTER.

  2. In the Privacy Information section box, change the fields as needed. For more information, see ALIAS Field Definitions.

  3. Press CTRL+S to save.
