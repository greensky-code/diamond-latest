About PXRUL

Use keyword PXRUL (Pre-Existing Condition Rule) to add or change pre-existing condition rules that can be applied at the line of business and group level. You can associate a pre-existing condition rule with specific groups in a line of business using keyword LBPEC (Line of Business Pre-Existing Conditions).

LBPEC pre-existing condition rules are considered "default" rules for the group and line of business in that DIAMOND applies LBPEC pre-existing condition rules only when no member-specific pre-existing condition rules apply.
