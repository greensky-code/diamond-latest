About THIST

Use keyword THIST (Tooth History) to store and maintain a history of all dental procedures performed on a member. Each tooth history record stores information on the tooth, arch, surface, and quadrant affected by the procedure.

DIAMOND automatically adds a tooth history record for each dental service claim detail line that you enter, whether it is entered online or through the DDS API. You can also manually enter tooth history records into THIST.
