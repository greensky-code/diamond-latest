Excluding Capitated Claims from COB

Instructions

You can exclude capitated claims from COB adjudication. This means that for capitated claims DIAMOND adjudication logic ignores the COB calculation method listed on the corresponding group detail record.

Instructions

Step

Comment

1. Determine the group and period for which you want to exclude capitated claims.

 

2. Select the corresponding Group Detail (GRUPD) record. In the field Exclude Capitated Claims select Professional, Institutional or Both as appropriate.

DIAMOND ignores related claims for COB adjudication. DIAMOND still displays the COB Warning Message Window and the Other Carrier Information Window in the normal manner.
