About DSPBN

Use keyword DSPBN (Display Benefit Accumulators) to show a member’s benefit information and current benefit status as of a specified date.

Information shown includes benefit rule IDs, benefit rule limits, expended benefit amounts, and remaining benefit amounts.

You can also select a specific benefit rule to display all of the member’s claims that have been processed against the benefit rule.
