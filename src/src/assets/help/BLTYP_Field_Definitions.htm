Bill Type

R

Three digit numeric code which may be defined according to UB-92, or have local, implementation specific meaning.

Manually entered.

Standard UB92 bill types:

First position

1 Hospital

2 Skilled Nursing

3 Home Health

4 Christian Science Hospital

5 Christian Science Extended Care

6 Intermediate Care

7 Clinic

8 Special Facility

9 Reserved for national use

Second position

1 Inpatient

3 Outpatient

Third position

0 Zero balance claim

1 Admit through discharge claim

2 Interim bill, first claim

3 Interim bill, continuation claim

4 Interim bill, final claim

5 Late charge to previous claim

6 Adjusted bill

7 Replacement of prior claim

8 Void or canceled claim

Description

O

Text description of the bill type.

Manually entered.

 

Inpatient/ Outpatient Indicator

R

Indicator of whether services being billed are for inpatient or outpatient.

Manually entered.

 

Place of Service

O

Place of service code with which the bill type is associated.

Selected from a predefined list

Place of service codes are maintained in PLSVC.
