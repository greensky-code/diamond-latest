Add a line of business pre-existing conditions rule

Use this procedure to add a line of business pre-existing conditions rule.

To add a line of business pre-existing conditions rule

  1. Press F3, type LBPEC, and then press ENTER.

  2. Fill in the fields on the window. For more information, see LBPEC Field Definitions.

  3. Press CTRL+S to save.
