Paying a claim to the dependent of a subscriber

Restrictions

Instructions

Frequently Asked Questions

Use the following procedure to pay a claim to the dependent of a subscriber.

Restrictions

The member you select must be the dependent of a subscriber, not the actual subscriber. Usually, dependents are indicated with a Person No. of 2 or higher in keyword MEMBR (Member Master).

Instructions

Step

Comment

1. In keyword INCLM, PSCLM, or DNCLM, display the non-final claim for which you want to pay the subscriber.

 

2. On the Topic menu, click Header File.

 

3. On the Special menu, click Pay Dependent.

The Claim Header Pay Dependent dialog box displays.

4. Click to select the Pay Dependent check box, and then click OK.

 

5. On the File menu, click Save.

A vendor record is created in keyword VENDR (Vendor Master) with the Vendor ID prefixed with "SD."

Frequently Asked Questions

None.
