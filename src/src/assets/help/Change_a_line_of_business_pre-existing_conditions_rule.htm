Change a line of business pre-existing conditions rule

Use this procedure to change a line of business pre-existing conditions rule.

To change a line of business pre-existing conditions rule

  1. Press F3, type LBPEC, and then press ENTER.

  2. In the Line of Business box, type the line of business code for which you want to change a rule. F5 lookup is available.

  3. In the PEC Rule ID picklist, click the PEC rule ID of the rule you want to change.

  4. Make the changes you want. For more information, see LBPEC Field Definitions.

  5. Press CTRL+S to save.
