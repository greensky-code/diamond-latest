About GRUPB

Use GRUPB to add, view and modify group premium billing rules, including who receives the bill, when and how often bills are produced, proration method, retroactivity, and grace period.

 

Important:

If you are using Super Group/Administrator/Group levels (GROUP), you must enter and maintain all Billing Information fields (except Common Billing Date and Billed Through Date) on the corresponding (Group Level = Group) GRUPB record. DIAMOND displays a warning message if you attempt to enter data in these fields on the associated GRUPB Super Group or Administrator level records.
