BLHST Field Definitions

Field Name

Req

Description

Source

Comments

Adjustment Amount

D

Total AR adjustments entered in keyword ARADJ (A/R Adjustments) since the previous invoice was run.

Displayed

DIAMOND may truncate the display of the currency amount.

Amount Billed

D

Invoice’s current Amount Billed, equal to total premium charges for the billing period plus retroactive adjustments and administrative fees.

Displayed

 

Annual Premium Income

D

Annualized family premium.

Displayed

The family composition is as of the end of the billing period, calculated by multiplying the monthly family premium by 12; includes administrative fees.

DIAMOND may truncate the display of the currency amount.

Balance Fwd

D

Amount carried from the prior billing period forward to the invoice being displayed.

Displayed

DIAMOND may truncate the display of the currency amount.

Batch ID

D

Batch ID of the A/R cash receipt.

Displayed

Originally entered as part of the cash receipt transaction.

Bill From

O

First date of a date range of billing information to be displayed.

Manually entered

If left blank, all billing information is displayed.

Bill From

D

First day of the invoice’s billing period.

Displayed

 

Bill Through

O

Last date of a date range of billing information to be displayed.

Manually entered

If left blank all billing history for the selected customer is displayed.

Bill Through

D

Last day of the invoice’s billing period.

Displayed

 

Billed Through Date

D

Last day of the invoice’s billing period.

Displayed

 

Company

D

General ledger company code.

Displayed

Originally entered as part of the cash receipt transaction.

Current Billed Amount

D

Current billed amount on the invoice, same as Amount Billed above.

Displayed

DIAMOND may truncate the display of the currency amount.

Customer ID

R

Valid Customer ID for whom billing information is to be displayed.

Selected from predefined list

Customer IDs are created by DIAMOND as a function of executing premium billing, or, rarely, by use of ARCUS.

Customer Type

R

Category of customer for whom billing information is to be displayed.

Selected from predefined list

Valid values are

Group

Combined

Individual

Description

D

Text description of the A/R cash receipt transaction.

Displayed

Originally entered as part of the cash receipt transaction.

GL Ref

D

Cross reference code representing a set of debit and credit accounts in the general ledger and associated with this A/R cash receipt.

Displayed

Originally entered as part of the cash receipt transaction.

Invoice #

D

Invoice number associated with the line of billing information for the selected customer.

Displayed

 

Invoice #

D

Invoice number of the bill on which the A/R cash receipt was reflected.

Displayed

Assigned by DIAMOND during billing.

Invoice Date

D

Date the invoice was run.

Displayed

 

Outstanding Balance

D

Amount owed by the customer on the invoice displayed.

Displayed

DIAMOND may truncate the display of the currency amount.

Payment Amount

D

Total amount of payments received and posted in keyword ARCSH since the previous invoice was run.

Displayed

DIAMOND may truncate the display of the currency amount.

Payment Due Date

D

Invoice due date.

Displayed

Payment due date is calculated according to the Due Date Rule and Calc Days from the Group Master.

Post Date

D

Date the A/R cash receipt batch was posted.

Displayed

Originally entered as part of the cash receipt transaction.

Statement Status

D

Cash receipts may be printed on customer invoices. This field displays the availability of this transaction for printing.

Displayed

Valid values are:

Not reflected on bill: Although available to print on an invoice, it has not yet been printed.

Reflected on bill—Unposted: The transaction is available to print because GPBIL Calculate has run, but that run has not yet been posted.

Reflected on bill—Posted:

Trans # or Trans Number

D

Transaction number of the A/R cash receipt.

Displayed

Originally entered as a part of the cash receipt transaction.

Displays up to 35 numeric characters.

Trans Amt

D

Dollar amount of the A/R cash receipt.

Displayed

Originally entered as part of the cash receipt transaction.

DIAMOND may truncate the display of the currency amount.

Transaction Amount

D

Dollar amount of the A/R cash receipt.

Displayed

Originally entered as part of the cash receipt transaction.

DIAMOND may truncate the display of the currency amount.

Transaction Date

D

Date the A/R cash receipt was entered.

Displayed

Originally entered as part of the cash receipt transaction.

Transaction Received Date

D

Date the cash receipt was received.

Displayed

Originally entered as part of the cash receipt transaction.

Transaction Type

D

Category of transaction.

Displayed

DIAMOND fills this field with information as entered in keyword ARCSH.

Standard values are:

Cash

Card

EFT

Check
