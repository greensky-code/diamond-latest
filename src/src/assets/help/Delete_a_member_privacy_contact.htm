Delete a member privacy contact

You cannot delete a member privacy contact. You can use keyword ALIAS (Alias/Responsible Party/Privacy) to blank out privacy contact fields Email Id and Fax Number, but you cannot blank out Contact Source or Distr. Method.

To blank out a member privacy contact's Email Id and Fax Number

  1. Press F3, type ALIAS, and then press ENTER.

  2. In the Contact Information section box, blank out the Email Id and Fax Number boxes.

  3. Press CTRL+S to save.
