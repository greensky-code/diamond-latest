About GROUP

Use keyword GROUP (Group Master) to add new employer and coverage groups and to maintain existing group information.

The term group refers to an organization (a group of subscribers) that purchases benefit plan coverage. For example, a group of people working for the same employer (company, school, union), Medicare members, or Medicaid members.

The group master table stores the name and address of the group, group contact information, and some information related to group premium billing.
