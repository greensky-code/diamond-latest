Adjust a claim detail line item

Use the following procedure to adjust a claim detail line item.

To adjust a claim detail line item

  1. In the claim detail window, display the detail line item you want to adjust.

  2. On the Special menu, click Adjust Line. A new detail line is created.

  3. Enter the quantity and financial adjustment in the appropriate fields. You can type a positive or negative adjustment. To adjust the accumulators for quantity or days limits, enter a negative quantity in the Qty box.

  4. Enter the corresponding reason code for each adjustment from the original claim detail line. DIAMOND will not make adjustments to the benefit accumulators for Dollar Amount limits unless the correct reason codes from the original claim line are entered.

  5. On the File menu, click Save.
