About CFMOD

Use CFMOD (Capitation Fund Model) to build and maintain models for use in calculating provider payments as a share of a specified fund of dollars. This type of fund may be established as part of a capitation arrangement, but otherwise is not associated with traditional capitation. The specified fund of dollars is known as a Cap/Special Fund (CFD) in DIAMOND.

A provider’s share of the fund is based on the provider’s payment arrangement and on the claims submitted. You use CFD models to reflect these payment arrangements with your providers. You can build a model and, if required, submodels to reflect each of your Cap/Special Fund agreements.

You use CFD models to run a batch job with keyword CFDST (Capitation Fund Distribution). During the batch job, DIAMOND uses this information to modify the net amount and Cap Fund Status of selected claim detail lines so that payments can be generated as part of the accounts payable cycle.
