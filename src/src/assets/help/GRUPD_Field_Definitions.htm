Payment Policy

O

COB payment policy for this group and effective period

Selected from a predefined list

Informational field only, no associated processing logic. You can use Payment Policy as a COB Hold/Deny determinant.

Payment Policy codes are maintained in CODES with system code type COBPYMT.

Predefined valid values:

Pay & Pursue: You normally allow DIAMOND to pay claims for this group. You then pursue potential payment recovery from another payor outside of DIAMOND, if appropriate.

Pursue & Pay: You normally have DIAMOND hold or deny claims for this group for this effective date period. You then pursue potential recovery from another payor, if appropriate. Once you receive a payment from another payor for a claim or determine another payor payment does not exist, you normally release the hold or deny conditions for the claim so DIAMOND can pay it.

Modified Pursue & Pay: For the DIAMOND Subscriber, you normally apply the Pay & Pursue policy. For all dependents, you apply follow the Pursue & Pay policy.

None: No formal policy exists for COB payments.
