About PROCP

Use keyword PROCP (Procedure Price) to add and change procedure code pricing. Each procedure code may have multiple procedure price records depending on the price schedule, price region, modifier code, and effective date.

You can also use a PROCP record to override a provider’s contract type, the percent of billed and the percent withhold, because the entries on the PROCP record take precedence over the entries on the PROVC (Type, Pct Of Billed, Pct Withhold) and/or the percent of billed on the member’s group detail record (GRUPD — Pct Billed).
