Act Adm Dt

O

Date of first admission.

Manually entered

Actual Admission Date. Date the member was originally admitted to the institutional provider (for example, hospital, SNF) or the origin date for other services (for example, first home health service).

If the claim entered is a continuation bill this may be the same date as the First Admitted.

LRD

O

Number of Lifetime Reserve Days used, if the beneficiary elects to use them. Medicare defines Lifetime Reserve Days as the 60 days available for inpatient services after 90 days have been used for a single episode of illness. Lifetime Reserve Days are not renewable.

Manually entered
