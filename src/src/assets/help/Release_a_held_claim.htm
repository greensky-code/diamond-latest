Release a held claim

Restrictions

Instructions

Frequently Asked Questions

You can release claim holds in two ways:

  • You can manually release a claim hold either by marking as historical or by deleting the hold reason record. These steps are described below.

  • You can also release multiple holds at once based on sets of determinants using a batch process. See Batch Claim Hold Release Overview User Documentation for more information.

Restrictions

None.

Instructions

Step

Comment

1. At the Main Menu, press F3.

 

2. In the Keyword box, do one of the following:

  • If you want to hold on institutional claim, then type INCLM, and then press ENTER.

  • If you want to hold a professional services claim, then type PSCLM, and then press ENTER.

  • If you want to hold a dental services claim, then type DNCLM, and then press ENTER.

 

3. On the Claim header window, display the claim you want to release.

 

4. On the Topic menu, click Hold Reasons.

 

5. Select the hold reason record and do one of the following:

  • To delete the record, on the File menu, click Delete.

  • To mark the record as historical, select the History check box.

If you delete a hold reason record, you lose that part of the hold history of the claim. Marking the record as historical makes the hold inactive while retaining the hold history. Hold history can be useful, for example, in answering inquiries about why a claim or claim line was not paid earlier.

A hold reason record cannot be modified when marked as historical. It can still be deleted.

6. If you need to re-price or re-adjudicate the claim, then on the Special menu, point to Pricing and Adjudication, and then click the appropriate command.

 

7. On the File menu, click Save.
