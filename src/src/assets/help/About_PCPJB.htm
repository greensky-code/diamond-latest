About PCPJB

Use PCPJB to request a batch job that executes the PCP Auto Assignment process. You can request a PCPAA job using selection criteria, including:

  • The date the member eligibility was added

  • The process that added the member eligibility record

  • The member’s line of business (one specific line of business, a range, or all lines of business)
