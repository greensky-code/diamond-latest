Manually hold an entire claim

Restrictions

Instructions

Frequently Asked Questions

Use the following procedure to hold an entire claim by entering a hold reason record for claim Line #000.

Restrictions

None.

Instructions

Step

Comment

1. At the Main Menu, press F3.

 

2. In the Keyword box, do one of the following:

  • If you want to hold on institutional claim, then type INCLM, and then press ENTER.

  • If you want to hold a professional services claim, then type PSCLM, and then press ENTER.

  • If you want to hold a dental services claim, then type DNCLM, and then press ENTER.

 

3. On the Claim header window, display the claim you want to manually hold.

 

4. On the Topic menu, click Hold Reasons.

 

5. Enter a hold reason record with claim Line #000 and the appropriate hold reason code.

DIAMOND automatically assigns a Source ID of Manual to differentiate manual holds from automatically assigned header holds (which also hold the entire claim). DIAMOND also assigns identifying information such as User ID and Date/Time.

6. If you need to re-price or re-adjudicate the claim, then on the Special menu, point to Pricing and Adjudication, and then click the appropriate command.

 

7. On the File menu, click Save.

 

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I manually hold a claim if it is already being held?

Yes, you can have multiple hold reason code records on a single claim. These may be multiple header hold records for line #000, multiple hold records for an individual claim detail line item, or a combination.
