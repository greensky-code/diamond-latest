About TRENT

Use keyword TRENT (Tax Reporting Entity) to specify the IRS tax ID and payer/transmitter information for each tax reporting entity, used to produce annual 1099-MISC tax forms.

The relationship between companies (established in COMPF) and taxable entities may be one-to-one, many-to-one, or many-to-many.

Taxable entity is the highest financial entity. Line of business is the highest member entity. There is no hierarchy between taxable entity and line of business. You can assign one line of business to multiple taxable entity records and you can assign one taxable entity record to multiple lines of business.
