Adjusting a Claim Detail Line

Restrictions

Instructions

Use the following procedure to adjust a claim detail line.

Restrictions

None.

Instructions

Step

Comment

1. From DNCLM, display the claim detail line you want to adjust.

 

2. On the Special menu, click Adjust Line.

DIAMOND will create a new detail line

3. Enter any quantity or financial adjustments in the appropriate fields.

DIAMOND will accept either a positive or negative adjustment.

To adjust the accumulators for quantity or days limits, enter a negative quantity in the Qty box.

4. Enter the corresponding reason code for each adjustment from the original claim detail line.

DIAMOND will not make adjustments to the benefit accumulators for Dollar Amount limits unless the correct reason codes from the original claim line are entered.

5. On the File menu, click Save.
