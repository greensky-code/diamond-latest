Re-apply a user-defined Hold rule to a claim

Restrictions

Instructions

Frequently Asked Questions

User-defined CLHLD claim header hold rules are applied when going from the claim header to detail, after the header has been saved. Follow the steps below if you want to re-apply user-defined hold rules for a selected claim, for example, if you made a change to claim header information or changed the hold rules.

Restrictions

None

Instructions

Step

Comment

1. At the Main Menu, press F3.

 

2. In the Keyword box, do one of the following:

  • If you want to re-apply a hold to an institutional claim, then type INCLM, and then press ENTER.

  • If you want to hold re-apply a hold to a professional services claim, then type PSCLM, and then press ENTER.

  • If you want to hold re-apply a hold to a dental services claim, then type DNCLM, and then press ENTER.

 

3. On the Claim header window, display the claim you want to re-apply a hold.

 

4. On the Special menu, click Re-Apply Claim Holds.

When the automatic hold logic is manually re-applied using Re-Apply Claim Holds, all existing automatic header holds are marked as historical.

Re-apply CLHLD hold rules before any lines have been finalized (Processing Status = F-Final) in order to hold the entire claim.

5. If you need to re-price or re-adjudicate the claim, then on the Special menu, point to Pricing and Adjudication, and then click the appropriate command.

 

6. On the File menu, click Save.

 

Frequently Asked Questions

None.
