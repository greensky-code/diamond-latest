Claim Holds – An Introduction

You can place a hold on a professional services, institutional, or dental claim. Typically, you hold a claim to prevent payment pending resolution of some problem with the claim.

You can hold entire claims and/or one or more specific claim detail lines on a single claim. Holding an entire claim means that you prevent any claim detail lines on the claim from being finalized and selected for payment by DIAMOND’s Accounts Payable module. Holding an individual claim detail line means that you prevent only that specific claim detail line from being finalized and selected for payment, assuming you permit partial payment of claims. That is, if you permit partial payment of claims (controlled by system parameter PARTIALPAY), other lines on the same claim that are not on hold can be finalized and selected for payment. If you do not permit partial payment of claims, holding an individual claim detail line prevents all lines on the claim from being finalized and selected for payment.

You can hold a claim using the following methods:

  • Manually hold an entire claim

  • Manually hold an individual claim detail line item

  • Use DIAMOND hold rules to hold individual claim detail line items (for example, you can hold all claims for a specific member)

  • Define table driven header hold rules, using any claim header field (except date fields), to hold entire claims (for example, you can hold entire claims for a particular vendor)

  • Activate holds generation from third party interfaces

  • Link a claim to an authorization which is on hold

  • Use benefit rules to hold claims which have particular attributes

  • Hold claims for members of groups which are ‘on hold’

  • Use holds from external products, such as Auto-Audit, which identify claims which require further investigation for billing anomalies

For more information, see CLHLD, Claim Hold Rules.

Viewing Claim Holds

You can view Claim Holds using the Hold Reason Display command on the Window menu in keywords INCLM, PSCLM, DNCLM, and CLDSP, or by the Hold Reasons command on the Topic menu in keywords INCLM, PSCLM, and DNCLM.
