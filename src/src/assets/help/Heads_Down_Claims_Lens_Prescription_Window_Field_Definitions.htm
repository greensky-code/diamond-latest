Heads Down Claims Lens Prescription Window Field Definitions

Field Name  Req Description Source Comments

OD Sphere (+ or -)

C

OD Sphere + or - indicator.

Selected from predefined list

Protected from entry if OD Sphere (Plano/Balance) will be entered.

Required if OD Sphere (value) will be entered.

OD Sphere (value)

C

OD Sphere value.

Manually entered

Protected from entry if OD Sphere (Plano/Balance) will be entered.

Required if OD Sphere (+ or -) is entered.

Enter up to a maximum of four digits in two place decimal format. Enter the decimal point when necessary. Digits following the decimal point must be in increments of .25. Maximum amount allowed is 99.75 (for +99.75 or -99.75).

OD Sphere (Plano/Balance)

C

OD Sphere plano or balance indicator.

Selected from predefined list

Protected from entry if OD Sphere (+ or -) and OD Sphere (value) are entered.

Valid values:

P = Plano

B = Balance

OD Cylinder (+ or -)

C

OD Cylinder (value)

C

OD Cylinder value.

Manually entered

Required if OD Cylinder (+ or -) is entered.

Enter up to a maximum of four digits in two place decimal format. Enter the decimal point when necessary. Digits following the decimal point must be in increments of .25. Maximum amount allowed is 99.75 (for +99.75 or -99.75).

OD Axis

C

OD Axis value.

Manually entered

Required if OD Cylinder (+ or -) and OD Cylinder (value) are entered.

Enter up to a maximum of three digits from 0 to 180.

OD Prism No. (1)

C

OD Prism No. (1) value.

Manually entered

Required if OD Prism Direc (1) will be entered.

Enter up to a maximum of four digits in two place decimal format. Enter the decimal point when necessary. Digits following the decimal point must be in increments of .25. Acceptable value range is from 01.00 to 20.00.
