About CLDSP

Use keyword CLDSP (Claims Display) to view a member’s professional service claims (PSCLM), institutional claims (INCLM), or dental claims (DNCLM) information. You can display all claims entered for the member or only those claims entered from a selected Date of Service to the present. You can also view all claim detail lines for a requested member.
