Enable validation of eligibility effective date against Medicare Part A effective date

  1. Press F3, type PARAM, and then press ENTER.

  2. In the Parameter ID checklist, select MEMMEDAEFF.

  3. In the Parameter 1 box, type Y.

  4. Press CTRL+S to save.

Note

This validation is only available for member eligibility periods with a Medicare + Choice line of business. The line of business on a member's eligibility record is a Medicare + Choice plan line of business when the corresponding LINBS record M+C Ind check box is selected.
