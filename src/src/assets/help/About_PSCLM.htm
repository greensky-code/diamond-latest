About PSCLM

Use keyword PSCLM (Professional Services Claims) to enter, update, price and adjudicate professional services claims, including primary care, specialist, vision, and other professional services.

Keyword PSCLM has multiple windows, including the claim header, which pertains to the entire claim, the claim detail, which itemizes each service provided.

Member, vendor, and provider data can be changed on a claim that has been entered in DIAMOND if the claim has not been finalized.
