About ARCUS

Use keyword ARCUS (Customer Maintenance) to add and update customers. A customer is the group or subscriber, which is financially responsible for premium payments. The identity of the customer depends on the Bill To selected on the Group Billing (GRUPB) window and the Group Type on the Group Master (GROUP) window. If the Group Type is 1-Group or 4-Annual Billing, the customer is the group. If the Group Type is 2-Individual or 3-Combined, the customer is the subscriber. (Group Types 5, Medicaid, and 6, Medicare Risk, do not link to premium billing.)

Most customer records are added and updated automatically each time premium billing is calculated—Premium Billing (GPBIL). However, when a new customer pays for coverage prior to being billed, a customer record must be entered manually so that the payment is reflected on the first premium billing invoice, and a customer record is required in order to post a cash receipt using Cash Receipts Entry (ARCSH).
