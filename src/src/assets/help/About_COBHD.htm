About COBHD

Use COBHD to build COB claim hold and deny rules to be applied during claims entry. COBHD rules supersede hold and deny conditions dictated by settings in the COB parameter COBCLAIMACTN.

Note  When you enter, modify or delete a COBHD rule and save, you must log out of DIAMOND and then log back in for the rule change to take effect.
