Lesser of Multiple Fee Schedule Calculations - Overview

Lesser of Multiple Fee Schedules

When pricing for the Lesser of Multiple Fee Schedules calculation method, the DIAMOND system attempts to find a match on the Procedure Code and/or Modifier and Price Region for all price schedules associated with the group record and the provider contract. If the system finds a matching price schedule, it then calculates the allowed amount for that price schedule by multiplying the price by the Percent Allowed specified, stores the resulting value for later use, then moves on to the next price schedule. Once all price schedules have been checked and calculated, the system determines the lowest allowed amount of all possible amounts calculated and applies that amount as the Allowed Amount.

For all calculation methods other than Lesser of Multiple Fee Schedules, the DIAMOND system evaluates price schedules until the first match is found. Once DIAMOND locates a match, it uses the information from that fee schedule to price.
