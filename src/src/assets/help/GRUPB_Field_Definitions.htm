GRUPB Field Definitions

Field Name  Req Description Source Comments

Across the Board

O

If checked, rates take effect immediately and remain in effect until the net Across the Board change, or until the next billing anniversary date (based on Effective Date in GRUPD).

Manually checked.

Used only if Rate Freeze Calc is Billing Start Date.

If checked, member ages are calculated as of the Billing Start Date, or, for members who are added after the Billing Start Date, the member effective date.

Admin CompCode

C

The general ledger company to which administrative fee revenue is posted.

Selected from a predefined list.

Required if Admin Fee is entered; otherwise not used.

Company Codes are maintained in COMPF.

Admin Fee

O

The amount of any administrative fee added to the calculated premium.

Manually entered.

Application of fee follows frequency and proration methods.

Admin Fee Level

C

Indicates whether administrative fee is calculated for the group as a whole or calculated and applied to each subscriber.

Selected from a predefined list.

Valid values are

Group

Subscriber: Required entry if Type is Individual or Combined.

Admin GL Ref

C

Cross reference to debit and credit accounts to which administrative fee revenue is posted.

Selected from a predefined list.

Required if Admin Fee is entered; otherwise not used.

GL Reference Codes are maintained in GLREF.

Adult Dep Age

O

Used in conjunction with Max No. Deps; the age at which a dependent is billed for as an adult. Applies only to rates that use Tiering Method Whole Family.

Manually entered.

Not used if Type is Annual Billing.

This field has no effect on Student Age on GRUPC.

Bill To

O

The recipient of bills.

Selected from a predefined list.

Applies only to groups with a Type of Annual Billing, Group, or Combined on the GROUP record.

Valid values are:

Administrator

Super Group

Group

Bill Type

D

Display of Group Type from the GROUP record.

System displayed.

 

Billed Through

D

The billed through date for groups with Type Group or Annual Billing

System generated; may be manually updated using menu command.

If group has not yet been billed, equals the day before the Group Contract Effective Date on GRUPC. For groups who’ve been billed, the last day of the last premium billing period that has been posted.

Common Billing Date

C

Billing start date used by Rate Freeze Calc.

Manually entered.

Date used by the system to calculate ages and rates.

Required if the group type is Group.

Optional if the group type is Combined.

Entry is not allowed if the group type is Annual or Individual.

If a Common Billing Date is not entered and the group type is Combined, then a billing start date for calculating ages and rates must be entered for each individual in the group on MEMBB.

Cycle

O

A batching method so that different groups can be billed at different times.

Manually entered.

If blank, this group will never be selected for billing because billing is initiated by entering a Cycle code.

Frequency

O

An indicator of the number of times a year this group is billed.

Selected from a predefined list.

If blank, group will never be billed.

Valid values are

Annually

Fortnightly (every 2 weeks)

Monthly

Quarterly

Semi-annually

Tenthly (12/10’s of the annual amount billed every month Sept-June)

Weekly

Grace

O

The number of days the group is allowed after the premium due date before a claim will be held.

Manually entered.

For example, if the number of days of grace is 4 and the premium is due on the 1st of the month, then the system would generate a group hold for a claim coming in on the 5th.

Group ID

R

Unique identifier of the Group.

Selected from a predefined list.

Group IDs are maintained in GROUP.

Matrix Def

C

The premium billing matrix used if Tiering Method is Subscriber or Whole Family.

Selected from a predefined list.

Matrix codes are maintained in PREMM.

Changes to Matrix Def potentially trigger group rate retroactivity, at your option. DIAMOND gives you the option to save changes with retroactivity or without retroactivity. For more information, see Retroactive Rate Adjustments.

Max No. Deps

O

Used in conjunction with Adult Dep Age; the maximum number of dependents who will be included in Tiering Method Whole Family.

Manually entered.

Not used if Type is Annual Billing.

A dependent is anyone who is neither Head of Household (i.e. subscriber) nor Spouse, according to the Relationship Code on the member’s MELIG record, and whose age is less than Adult Dep Age.

If field is blank, dependents are not included in premium calculations.

Paid Through

D

The paid through date for groups with Type Group or Annual Billing.

System generated; may be manually updated using menu command.

Calculated as a by-product of Cash Receipts Entry Posting.

Pct of Matrix

C

Percentage applied to amounts calculated according to entry in Matrix Def.

Manually entered.

Changes to Pct of Matrix potentially trigger group rate retroactivity, at your option. DIAMOND gives you the option to save changes with retroactivity or without retroactivity. For more information, see Retroactive Rate Adjustments.

Proration

O

The method used to calculate partial premiums depending on the day of the month on which the member’s eligibility changes.

Selected from a predefined list.

If Frequency is Weekly or Fortnightly, proration must be Daily. If billing start date is not the first of the month, proration must be Daily.

If Group Type is Annual Billing, no proration is used and field is not used.

Valid values are

None: A full month’s premium is charged for every member regardless of start or termination dates

Front/Back End Wash: A full month’s premium is charged for members whose start dates are from the 1st and 15th of the month OR are terminated between the 16 and the last day of the month. No premium is charged for members whose start dates are between the 16th and the last day of the month, OR who are both added and terminated between the 16th and the last day of the month, OR who are terminated between the 1st and the 15th of the month. A half month premium is charged for members who are added and then terminated between the 1st and the 15th.

Half Monthly: A full month’s premium is charged for members who start between the 1st and the 15th, or are terminated between the 16th and the last day of the month. A half-month’s premium is charged for members who are either added between the 16th and the last day of the month, OR are terminated between the 1st and the 15th, OR are added and terminated between the 1st and the 15th, OR are added and terminated between the 16th and the last day of the month.

Daily: Members are charged a premium calculated by dividing the number of days the member is eligible during the month by the total number of days in the month, multiplied by the monthly premium.

Rate 1-5

R

Monthly premium amounts associated with each step if 1-5 Step or Per Capita is the tiering method.

If Type is Annual Billing, this is the annual premium.

Manually entered.

For ‘Step’ methods, amounts apply to subscriber units; if Per Capita, amounts apply to each member.

Changes to Rates 1-5 potentially trigger group rate retroactivity, at your option. DIAMOND gives you the option to save changes with retroactivity or without retroactivity. For more information, see Retroactive Rate Adjustments.

Rate Freeze Calc

R

Indicator of how the as-of date for calculation of member ages and rates is determined.

Selected from a predefined list.

If Type is Annual Billing, system generates Billing Period Begin in this field.

Valid values are

Billing Period Begin: Member ages and rates are calculated as of the start date of the billing period.

Billing Start Date: Member ages and rates are calculated as of the Common Billing Date, or, if none, each member’s Billing Begin Date on MEMBB.

Rate Modified On

D

Displays the last date when any GRUPB rate information was modified.

System generated.

This field is updated for changes to GRUPB fields Tiering Mthd, Rate 1-5, Matrix Def, and Pct of Matrix.

Report Print Format

O

The format used by the implementation specific report writing software to print the premium bill.

Manually entered.

 

Retro Months

O

Maximum number of months (1-24) prior to the billing period that are considered for calculation of member eligibility retroactive billing adjustments.

Manually entered.

Optional field that is not used if Type is Annual Billing.

If group type is Group, enter the number of retroactive months applying to the whole group.

If group type is Individual or Combined, enter the retroactive months that apply to the dependents.

DIAMOND performs customer retroactivity calculations (based on the number of months entered) after the initial customer premium bill has been calculated and posted using GPBIL.

If you leave this field blank, DIAMOND does not make any member eligibility retroactive adjustments during the premium billing process. DIAMOND still performs group rate retroactivity if applicable.

See Retroactive Adjustments for more information.

Tiering Method

R

Pre-defined standard ways that health plans categorize groups and subscribers according to family characteristics in order to calculate premiums.

Selected from a predefined list.

Tiering methods are manually selected from hard coded values.

If Type is Annual Billing, the 1 Step Method must be used.

1 Step Method: A single rate applies to every subscriber, regardless of the number of members in the family.

2 Step Method:

  • rate step 1 applies to single subscribers

  • rate step 2 applies families of 2 or more members

3 Step Method:

  • rate step 1 applies to single subscribers

  • rate step 2 applies to families of 2 members

  • rate step 3 applies to families of 3 or more members

4 Step Method:

  • rate step 1 applies to single subscribers;

  • rate step 2 applies to families of subscriber + spouse or domestic partner;

  • rate step 3 applies to families of subscriber+2 or more dependents (regardless of whether there is a spouse or domestic partner);

  • rate step 4 applies to families of subscriber + non-spouse/non-domestic partner dependent.

5 Step Method:

  • rate step 1 applies to single subscribers

  • rate step 2 applies to families of subscriber + spouse or domestic partner

  • rate step 3, subscriber + non-spouse/non-domestic partner dependent

  • rate step 4, subscriber + spouse + one or more non-spouse/non-domestic partner dependents

  • rate step 5, families of subscriber + 2 or more non-spouse/non-domestic partner dependents

Per Capita Method: A single rate is applied to each member (per head)

Subscriber Method: Matrix applies at the subscriber level

N/A: tiering method not applicable

Whole Family: Matrix applies at the member level, up to the maximum number of dependents entered in the field above.

Changes to Tiering Method potentially trigger group rate retroactivity, at your option. DIAMOND gives you the option to save changes with retroactivity or without retroactivity. For more information, see Retroactive Rate Adjustments.

Use EFT

O

Indicator of whether group uses electronic funds transfer to pay premium bills.

Manually checked.

If selected, menu command D/C Information maintains account information.
