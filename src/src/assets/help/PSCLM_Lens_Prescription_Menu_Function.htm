Selected from predefined list.

The ten OD fields are repeated as a group in the Lens Prescription window. There is one group of OD fields for each eye.

The following four OD fields are used to define the prism for an eye:

  • Prism No. (1)

  • Prism Direc (1)

  • Prism No. (2)

  • Prism Direc (2)

Prism direction combinations (1 and 2) are invalid if they contain values as follows:

  • Up and Up

  • Down and Down

  • In and In

  • Out and Out

  • Up and Down

  • In and Out

  • Down and Up

  • Out and In

For values in decimal places, increments must be .25. Maximum sphere values are 99.75.
