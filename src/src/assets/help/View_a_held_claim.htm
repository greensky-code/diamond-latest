View a held claim

Frequently Asked Questions

Use the following procedure to view claim header holds and claim detail line holds for institutional, professional services, or dental services claims.

Restrictions

None.

Instructions

Step

Comment

1. Open the INCLM, PSCLM, or DNCLM claim for which you want to view claim holds in INCLM, PSCLM, DNCLM, or CLDSP.

The claim header includes a field indicating the presence of active header holds only. Claim detail lines include a field reflecting the presence of active detail holds only. These indicators do not reflect the presence of holds that have been marked as historical.

2. Do one of the following:

  • If you want to view Hold Reason codes for all claim line items, then on the Window menu, click Hold Reason Display.

  • If you want to view or edit the Hold Reason code(s) for a selected claim line item, then on the Topic menu, click Hold Reasons.

The Topic Hold Reasons is not available in keyword CLDSP.

These commands show both active and historical holds.

3. View the claim header holds and claim detail line item holds.

Claim header holds are identified with claim line #000. Claim detail line holds are listed by claim detail line number.

See INCLM, PSCLM, or DNCLM User Documentation for field descriptions of these holds indicator fields and for all fields included in the Topic Hold Reasons and the Window menu option Hold Reason Display.

Frequently Asked Questions

None.
